Google search filters and operators

  • ext:[file extension] - to search for a specific file type
  • before:[YYYY-MM-DD]
  • after:[YYYY-MM-DD]
  • site:[site] - to search in a specific site
  • inurl:[word] - to search for a word in the url
  • cache:[website] - to enter the website from the cache(for when it's down and you visited it before)
  • intitle:[word] - to search for something in the <title> HTML tag
  • [word1] | [word2] - to search for word1 OR word2
  • -[word] - to search for the absence of the word
*note: this is not a complete list
